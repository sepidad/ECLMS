# Contract Renewal Sequence

**Document ID:** SEQ-005

---

# Purpose

Defines renewal of expiring contracts.

---

# Trigger

Renewal policy or user request.

---

# Main Flow

1. Renewal initiated.
2. Eligibility validated.
3. Workflow assigned.
4. Approval completed.
5. Renewal created.
6. Audit recorded.
7. Notifications sent.

---

# Rules

- Renewal creates new lifecycle period.
- Historical lifecycle remains preserved.
